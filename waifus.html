Scathach

Scathach is a limited lancer (lol). She has 2 hits on her two quick cards, 3 hits on her one arts card, 6 hits on her two buster cards, and 7 hits on her extra attack. Her first skill, Wisdom of Dún Scáith, has an 80% Chance of increasing own critical damage for 3 turns and increasing own critical star absorption for 3 turns and will grant evade for 1 turn. Her second skill, Primeval Rune, increases one allies' quick performace for 1 turn. Her third skill, God Slayer, increases damage to divine or undead enemies for 1 turn.

Musashi

Musashi is a limited saber (lol). She has 3 hits on her one quick card, 3 hits on her one arts card, and 2 hits on her three buster cards, and 4 hits on her extra attack. Her first skill, Fifth Form, doubles the amount of hits on each of her cards for 1 turn. Her second skill, Empyrean Eye, ignores invincibilty and increases buster performance for 1 turn. Her third skill, Nothingness, grants invincibilty, increases star generation for 1 turn and removes Musashi's debuffs.

Ibuki Douji

Ibuki Douji is a limited saber (lol). She has four hits on her one quick card, 3 hits on one arts card, 3 hits on three buster cards, and 5 hits on her extra attack. Her first skill, Physical Strength of Natural Surroundings, increases her own attack for 3 turns and charges her NP guage. Her second skill, Eight Channels Surging Waves, increases her buster performance and defence for 3 turns and gains critical stars. Her third skill, Defiled Fingertips, increases her critical damage and damage against undead enemies for 3 turns and seals an enemy's NP for 1 turn.

Jeanne D'Arc (archer)

Jeanne D'Arc (archer) is a limited archer (lol). She has 4 hits on one quick card, 2 hits on two arts cards, 2 hits on two buster cards, and 5 hits on her extra attack. Her first skill, Endless Enjoy Summer!, increases arts performance for 3 turns and grants Invincibility for 2 attacks, 3 turns. Her second skill, Waterfront Saintess (Dolphin), charges her NP gauge, increases her NP damage for 3 turns, and increases her critcal damage on waterside battlefields for 3 turns. Her third skill, Servant Cheer!, increases party damage for 3 turns and further increases good aligned servants' attack for three turns.
