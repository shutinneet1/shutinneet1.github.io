Are you a new player to FGO and know nothing about the game? Well I'm not sure why you're here, but either way I'll offer some tips.

Some general advice: classes such as Riders and archers will generate more stars. The more hits a servant has on a card, the more damage, NP gain, and stars they generate.

And most importantly: Gacha rates are hell. Exploit friends for servants you need lol.

Supports

Merlin

Merlin is a buster support servant, meaning he is efficent when placed in a team with Buster servants. He has three hits on one quick card, two hits on three arts cards, five on one buster card, and six hits on his extra attack. His first skill, Dreamlike Chrisma rank A, increases the attack for all party members, while his third skill, Hero Creation rank Ex, increases damage from a single servant's buster card and their max health for three turns. His NP recovers 1000 health for all servants, increases all Servant's NP guage by 5%, and gains 5 stars every turn for 5 turns.

Skadi

Skadi is a quick support servant. She has four hits with two quick cards, three hits with two arts cards, and four hits with one buster card, and 5 hits on her extra attack. She is best in a quick team. Her first skill, Primeval Rune, increases one ally's Quick performance for 3 turns and their critical damage of Quick Cards for 3 turns. Her second skill, Shivering Blizzard, reduces all enemies' defense for 3 turns and their their critical attack chance for 3 turns. Her third skill, Allfather's Wisdom, increases the NP gauge of one servant.

Tamamo No Mae

Tamamo is a arts support servant. She has three hits on one quick card, five hits on three arts cards, one hit on one buster card, and four hits on her extra attack. Her first skill, Witchcraft, drains the enemy's NP gauge by one. Her second skill, Shapeshift, increases her own defence by 30% for one turn and further increases her defence for three turns. Her third skill, Fox's Wedding, increases the arts performance of one servant for three turns.

Zhuge Liang (Waver)

Waver is a general support servant with two hits on his one quick card, one hit on his three arts cards, one hit on his buster card, and three hits on his extra attack. His first skill, Discerning Eye, increases one ally's critical damage for 3 turns and charges their NP gauge by 30%. His second skill, Advice of the Strategist, increases party's defense for 3 turns, reduces party's damage taken for 3 turns, charges party's NP gauge by 10%. His third skill, Command of the Strategist, increases party's attack for 3 turns, increases party's damage for 3 turns, charges party's NP gauge by 10%.

Chen Gong

Chen Gong is a support caster with three hits on his one quick card, four hits on his three arts card, and one hit on his one buster card. Chen Gong's first skill, Scapegoat, has a 500% Chance to draw attention of all enemies to one ally by 300% for 1 turn and reduces all enemies' critical attack chance for 3 turns. Chen Gong's second skill, Advice of the Strategist, increases party's defense for 3 turns, reduces party's damage taken for 3 turns, charges party's NP gauge by 10%. Cheng Gong's third skill, Desire of Strategist, increases one ally's Buster performance for 1 turn, increases Class-Berserker-Gold.png Berserker ally's critical damage for 1 turn, increases Berserker ally's Max HP for 1 turn.

Artoria (Caster)/ Castoria

Castoria is a support caster. She has three hits with one quick card, three hits on her three arts cards, three hits on her buster card, and five hits on her extra attack. Her first skill, Charisma of Hope, increases party attack for three turns and charges all allies' NP gauges by gauge. Her second skill,Protection of Vivian, charges one allies' NP gauge, and increases the party's NP gain for three turns. Castoria's third skill, Caliburn: Sword of Selection, increases one ally's Arts performance for 3 turns, increases their damage against Threat to Humanity enemies for 3 turns, grants them Invincibility for 1 turn.
